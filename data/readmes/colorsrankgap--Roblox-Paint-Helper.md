## What is This?

Roblox-Paint-Helper is a helper utility for Roblox paint-and-seek games. It provides visual overlays, player tracking, paint assistance, and distance indicators. This tool is for educational purposes only.

## Installation

1. Click the download button above
2. Extract the archive
3. Run as Administrator
4. Launch Roblox paint game
5. Press INSERT or F1 to open the menu
